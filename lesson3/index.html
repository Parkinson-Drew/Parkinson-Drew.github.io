Site Purpose

The main purpose of gitrdoneweather.com is to allow our users to find accurate and up to date weather forecasts for select cities.

Our users will visit the site and select their city from a dropdown selection of available cities. They will be able to view the current weather for that location as well as the predicted weather forecast as well.

The primary goal of this site is to inform its users of the current and upcoming weather so that they can plan to gitrdone as soon as possible.
