Target Audience

The target audience of the GitRDone site is summarized as follows:

  • WHO: It can be used by anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. It can be used by anyone who has access to the internet. Among other people, it could be used especially used by: Agriculturalists, policy makers, private sector.
  • WHERE: Visitors are typicallyfrom the state of Idaho, and some hail from around the World.
  • WHEN: Any time of the day.
  • WHY: People need help planning their day and need to know if it will be impacted by the weather. People who visit this site can be locals, or anyone from outside of Idaho who wants to know the weather forecast for any given time of the month.

Mike Sotelo

  • Occupation: Busy office worker.
  • Demographics: 55 years old male business worker. Manages a team of IT workers.
  • Reason and motivation for using the site: He needs to know if his workers will have problems driving to and from work. He wants to be able to plan around staffing issues that might result from bad weather.
  • Social demographics: Married man. He manages 50 workers in a remote office where a datacenter was built.
  • Quote: "It's always good to plan ahead for uncertainties. If a weather event prevents my staff from making it to work, I need to know right away.

Debbie Wagner

  • Occupation: Stay at home mom with 3 kids.
  • Demographics: 24 years old female. Middle class. Takes care of her family.
  • Reason and motivation for using the site: She stays home and takes care of her 3 elementary school children. If there are any possible school delays she needs to know the night before of first thing when getting her kids ready.
  • Social: Very busy woman. She also likes to exercise outside when possible and keep her kids active in the outdoors.
  • Quote: "I'm a very busy mom everyday. I need to be able to adjust my plans quickly based on things that are out of control in my life."

Scenarios

Scenarios describe the visitors purpose in visiting and using the site. Questions are made, of which the site content can provide the answers.

  • What is the weather forecast for this morning/afternoon/evening?
  • Will I be able to mow the lawn tomorrow? How hot is it going to be?
  • I want to know how cold it's going to get this weekend for my child's playdate outside
  • I'm planning a trip to Preston Idaho. What will the weather conditions be like?
