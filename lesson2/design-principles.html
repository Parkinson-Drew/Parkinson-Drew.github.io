WHITE SPACE and CLEAN DESIGN

REI

www.rei.com

The REI website shows a nice example of the use of white space and a clean design. The spacing is very easy on the eyes and the layout of the text allows you to quickly find links and products.

CONTRAST

Codepen.io

codepen.io

Codepen shows how contrast makes a difference in being able to read text on a website. The black background with light or white text areas is very visually appealing and helps to emphasize the text more.

RULE of THIRDS

Target

www.target.com

Target's home page shows off the rule of thirds when it is displaying products so you are quickly drawn to the images and allowed to see lots of choices all at once.
